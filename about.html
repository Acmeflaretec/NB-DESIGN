About Us

NB Group of Companies
'Creativity for Growth'

The NB Group of Companies is a vibrant, forward-thinking organization committed to delivering innovative educational solutions. Founded by the visionary entrepreneur Muhammed Nabeel P A, the company fosters creativity, innovation, and excellence across all its ventures. Under the umbrella of the NB Group, the first venture, NB Design Solutions, was established in July 2020.

This company offers cutting-edge and professional design services customized to meet clients' unique needs. With a reputation for creative solutions and unwavering client satisfaction, NB Design Solutions has rapidly gained recognition in the industry. Building on this success, the company has expanded its horizons with the launch of NB Skill Hub. This venture focuses on two key areas: Creative Education and Language Training. The Creative Education initiative aims to empower aspiring designers by providing top-notch graphic design courses that equip individuals with the relevant skills needed to excel in the ever-evolving creative industry. Through this venture, the company nurtures talent, inspires innovation, and drives creativity within the industry. In addition, the comprehensive Language Training Institute empowers individuals to communicate confidently and effectively in today's globalized world. The academy adopts a practical approach to language learning, enabling individuals to overcome linguistic barriers and embrace diverse cultural interactions. Currently offering English language training, NB Group plans to expand to include other languages in the future, further enhancing individuals' communication abilities.

Beyond design-related ventures and language training, NB Group of Companies also launched NB Store. NB Store is the ultimate destination for fashion and accessories, offering a wide range of products that cater to personal style and allow individuals to express their unique individuality. From watches, shoes, belts, and spectacles to cosmetics and trendy t-shirts, NB Store prioritizes customer satisfaction. Its friendly and knowledgeable staff are always ready to assist customers in finding the perfect product, whether for themselves or as a gift. With a diverse range of products, NB Store ensures that customers can find something that suits their needs and preferences.

Driven by a mission to provide high-quality products, services, and training at affordable rates, the NB Group of Companies aims to make a positive impact, empower individuals, and inspire young entrepreneurs to pursue their dreams. With a vision for continued expansion and a commitment to creating opportunities for individuals to acquire valuable skills, the NB Group strives to leave a lasting impact on the design solutions, creative education, and retail sectors. Under the leadership of a talented and determined founder, the NB Group of Companies is poised for success and recognition in its respective industries. With a strong focus on innovation and a dedication to providing creative and educational solutions, the company aspires to shape the future of design and language education. The NB Group remains committed to delivering exceptional services, training, and products across all its endeavours.

Profile Summary

Muhammad Nabeel P.A. is a highly motivated and visionary entrepreneur who leads the N.B. Group of Companies, a dynamic and innovative conglomerate. With a passion for design and language education, he has successfully established a series of start-up ventures under the N.B. Group of Companies umbrella, focusing on providing creative and educational solutions. Despite his young age, Nabeel has demonstrated exceptional talent and determination, making significant strides in various industries.

Background

Muhammad Nabeel P.A. embarked on his entrepreneurial journey on July 22, 2020, driven by his passion for design and language education. He established N.B. Design Solutions, a company that offers innovative and professional design services tailored to meet clients' unique needs. Drawing from his success, Nabeel is in the process of launching N.B. Creative Education, an initiative aimed at offering top-notch graphic design courses to aspiring designers, equipping them with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the creative industry. Additionally, he envisions establishing N.B. Global Language Academy, providing comprehensive and practical language training to empower individuals to communicate confidently and effectively in today's globalized world, with plans to expand the training to include other languages in the future.

Nabeel's entrepreneurial endeavours are driven by his desire to make a positive impact, empower individuals, and inspire young entrepreneurs to pursue their dreams.

With his exceptional talent, determination, and commitment to providing innovative solutions, Muhammad Nabeel P.A. is leading the N.B.
